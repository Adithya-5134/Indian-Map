Cultural Heritage & Nature

Chhattisgarh is home to **diverse tribal communities** that have preserved their traditions for centuries. The **Bastar Tribal Dance** is an integral part of festivals and celebrations.

Nicknamed the **'Rice Bowl of India'**, Chhattisgarh is also famous for its lush green landscapes, dense forests, and scenic waterfalls, including **Chitrakote Falls**, often compared to Niagara Falls.
