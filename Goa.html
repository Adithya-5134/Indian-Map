Culture & Heritage

Goa, influenced by **Portuguese architecture and traditions**, boasts **centuries-old churches**, vibrant festivals like **Carnival**, and delicious seafood cuisine. The **Basilica of Bom Jesus**, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, houses the mortal remains of **St. Francis Xavier**.

Basilica of Bom Jesus, Goa

Nature & Adventure

Beyond its golden beaches, Goa is home to **lush green forests, waterfalls, and spice plantations**. The stunning **Dudhsagar Waterfalls**, located on the Goa-Karnataka border, is one of India's tallest waterfalls.

Dudhsagar Waterfalls, Goa

Entertainment & Nightlife

Goa is famous for its **vibrant nightlife, casinos, and beach parties**. The state has both floating and land-based casinos, offering entertainment and gaming experiences.
